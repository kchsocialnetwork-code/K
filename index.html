I wrote something for you.

Oye…

Nanna aaa…

The word… the emotion it carries… I never thought in my life I would call someone “nanna” again after my father.

Thank you for allowing me to call you that.

At first, I said it unknowingly… But later, I realized… the way you are sometimes… your gestures… made me feel like him.

Maybe… I was always quietly looking for my father’s love in my man. But you didn’t just remind me of him… you made me feel it again.

For that… I will always feel blessed.

I wanted to write this long ago… but I waited to tell you this on your birthday.

“Nuvvu puttinadaniki oka ardam undi… ahh ardam neney.”

I want you to feel blessed for having me in your life… and meaning to your birth.

Honestly… I wasn’t even looking for love. Not for a person… not for anything.

I was tired of people… tired of emotions… tired of everything.

I believed even if someone loved me… I wouldn’t be able to love them back.

I knew I wouldn’t let anyone come close… I wouldn’t let anyone touch my thoughts… my soul…

But still… there were moments… when everything felt heavy… especially without my father…

And somewhere inside… I questioned — “Don’t I deserve someone?”

That’s when I left everything to Shivayya.

I said… if I deserve love… send me someone.

And then… you came.

I still believe… you were sent into my life.

Everything just happened… naturally… like it was meant to.

I strongly feel… he will take this till the end.

Even the thought of losing this… I can’t take it.

Niku em problem ochina… I worry… more than I should.

I’m honestly scared of how much I love you.

I just want you close… I want to take care of you… I want to be there when you’re tired…

I’ve never felt this way for anyone.

Nuvvu undu kanna natho… naku inkem odhu…

Nuvvu eppudu na pakkana undu chalu… chaalu…

Naku e jeevitham motham bathikesta.

— Yours ❤️
